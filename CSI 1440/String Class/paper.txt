BREAKING NEWS ASSOCIATED PRESS

WASHINGTON, Feb. 7  The nation’s
unemployment rate dropped to 5.7
percent in January as businesses
added 143,000 new jobs, a shot of
good news for an ailing economy.

THE INCREASE in payroll jobs, mostly
in the retail area, was the largest
since November 2000, said Friday’s
Labor Department report. The overall
rate dropped by 0.3 percentage point
from the 6 percent rate in December
that matched an 8-year high.
